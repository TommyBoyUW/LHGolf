Left-Handed Champions
The Elite 7%

Left-handed champion golfers are a unique and often celebrated group in the sport, showcasing exceptional talent and skill on the greens. While left-handedness is generally rare in the population—affecting only about 7% of people—its prevalence among elite golfers is even less common. This rarity adds an intriguing layer to the sport, as left-handed players often face distinctive challenges, from equipment availability to adapting to a predominantly right-handed world.
