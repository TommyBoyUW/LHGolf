Checklist

Weight on toes not heels.
Head down from back swing to ball contact.
Keep back straight.
Twist hips through swing.
Place headcover under trail arm.
Practice on level ground first.
Take half swing to focus on accuracy.
Smooth swing, do not try to kill it.
